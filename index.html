Why it stands out

Movement-first arcade design

See all features

Dash + phase

Two core abilities define how you route, escape, attack, and recover. The game gets its identity from speed and control, not from slow maze crawling.

Procedural pressure

Each run pushes deeper into harder floors with changing hazards, tighter lines, and more reasons to optimize your movement.

Custom tools

Map maker, custom sound tools, emoji visuals, and unlockable hats give the game more personality than a basic score attack setup.
