What stands out

Movement is the identity.

Crum Run is not a slow maze crawl. Dash and phase define route changes, escapes, offensive pressure, and the feel of every run.

Run variety

Hazards, powerups, and random pressure.

Hot floors, lava, goo, moving walls, upgrades, and boss encounters keep runs from flattening into the same pattern.

More than score attack

Build, customize, share.

Map maker tools, hats, sound tweaking, and emoji-based visuals give the game a stronger identity than a barebones arcade loop.
