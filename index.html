Manual

Core game information without forcing people to dig through the store page

Objective and run flow

Runs are built around navigating procedural mazes efficiently while surviving rising pressure and pushing to higher floors. The game rewards movement control, cleaner routing, and faster execution as difficulty increases.

Movement abilities

Dash and phase are the two defining skills. Dash boosts speed and launches the player forward. Phase lets you push through walls. Used well, they create faster lines, safer escapes, and stronger offensive pressure in PvP.

Powerups and run variety

Powerups add more than passive bonuses. Runs can include effects and weapons such as fire trail, mega shield, rapid blaster, snake cannon, and shotgun, which shifts how floors feel from run to run.

Hazards and obstacles

Floors can include goo, hot coals, lava, moving walls, fog of war, and boss encounters. These elements force route changes instead of letting every floor play the same.

Controls and support

The current release supports full key remapping, USB gamepads, adjustable display settings, keyboard and mouse input, and local multiplayer for one to two players.

Demo versus full version

The free demo includes the core gameplay, but progression ends at floor 11 and some customization features stay locked. The full version removes those limits and unlocks saving, loading, and sharing for custom maps, sound settings, and emoji sets.
