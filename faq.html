FAQ

What you get in the demo, what unlocks in full.

Quick answers for platform, input, multiplayer, creator tools, customization, and what the free demo does and does not include.

What is Crum Run?

A fast arcade maze game for Windows built around dash, phase, route optimization, escalating runs, local multiplayer, and customization.

What does the free demo include?

The demo includes the core gameplay loop. Runs stop at floor 11, some customization and export features stay locked, and PvP is reduced compared with the full version.

What unlocks in the full version?

Unlimited floor and level progress, full map save/load/share, full DSP save/load, and full emoji-set save/load once unlocked through play.

How many players does it support?

One or two local players. You can play solo, couch co-op, or local PvP.

What inputs are supported?

Keyboard, mouse, and gamepad are supported. The game also supports full key remapping.

Can I customize the game?

Yes. The game includes hats, emoji-based visuals, and a custom sound scheme system. The full version expands save/load and sharing options.

Is there a map maker?

Yes. You can create maze maps, test them, and in the full version save, load, and share them.

Where do I buy or download it?

Everything goes through the official itch.io page. This website points there on purpose instead of trying to fake a store.
