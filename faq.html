FAQ

Quick answers before you play.

Platform, price, demo limits, controls, requirements, and where to get the game.

What platform is Crum Run on?

Crum Run is currently listed as a downloadable game for Windows.

How much is the full game?

The full version is listed at $4.99 USD or more on itch.io.

Is there a free demo?

Yes. A free demo is available and includes the core gameplay loop.

What does the demo limit?

The demo run ends at floor 11, some customization/export features stay locked, and PvP is reduced compared with the full version.

How many players and what inputs are supported?

The public store listing shows 1–2 local players with keyboard, mouse, and gamepad support, plus configurable controls.

What are the minimum requirements?

Windows 10, dual-core CPU, 4 GB RAM, integrated graphics, and 200 MB of storage.

Demo vs full version

Know the difference quickly.

Free demo

  • Core gameplay available now
  • Run ends at floor 11
  • Some map, emoji, and DSP features locked
  • Reduced PvP setup

Full game

  • Unlimited floor and level progression
  • Save, load, and share custom maps
  • Save, load, and share DSP settings
  • Save, load, and share unlocked emoji sets
